About Us

This is a project made by four amateur developers for the 2020 ecx hackx hackathon.
This solution was inspired byn the fact that due to the coronavirus pandemic many people were not allowed to see their doctors.
This lead to a reduce in doctor patient appointments and uptick in the severity of the illnesses because of the fear of catching the coronavirus
This project helps take care of the needs of people by allowing patients to communicate with their doctors over the internet and get diagnosis and expert recommendations
This also allows them to book specific in person appointments to avoid wait lines.
